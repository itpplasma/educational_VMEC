179  ! COMPUTE CMNS AND THE COEFFICIENTS OF T+- IN EQ (A14 AND A13) IN J.COMP.PHYS PAPER (PKM)
180  ! NOTE: HERE, THE INDEX L IN THE LOOP BELOW IS THE SUBSCRIPT OF T+-. THEREFORE,
181  ! L = 2L' + Kmn (L' = INDEX IN EQ. A14, Kmn = |m-n|), WITH LMIN = K AND LMAX = Jmn == m+n.
182  !
183  ! THE FOLLOWING DEFINITIONS PERTAIN (NOTE: kmn <= L <= jmn):
184  !
185  ! F1 = [(L + jmn)/2]! / [(jmn - L)/2]! == [(jmn + kmn)/2 + L']!/[(jmn - kmn)/2 + L']!
186  !
